Combat System

How to Play

BattleForge is a one-on-one tactical duel. Each round, you and the Dark Mage simultaneously weigh risk vs. reward — attack for reliable damage, defend to absorb the next blow, or spend precious Skill Points on a devastating special move.

⚔️
ATTACK

A direct strike against the enemy. Consistent and free — your most reliable action.

15 – 25 DMG · NO COST
🛡️
DEFEND

Brace for the enemy's attack. Halves incoming damage this turn and restores a small amount of HP.

½ DAMAGE · +5 HP REGEN
✨
SPECIAL

Channel raw power into a single devastating blow. High risk, high reward — manage your SP wisely.

30 – 45 DMG · COSTS 10 SP
Pro Tips
🎯Defend when your HP drops below 30 — the enemy hits harder when you're low.
⚡Spend Special early and often — an SP pool left unspent is wasted potential.
🧠The Dark Mage uses Defend too. Bait it, then Attack twice in a row.
❤️Consecutive Defends recover HP and can turn the tide of a losing battle.
